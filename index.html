About Us

Who Am I?

Hi I'm Lucy Stent , I am 28 and in my first year of a PhD focusing on machine learning. I have just completed a MSc in AI for Games at Falmouth University.

I was awarded for academic achievement from Women in Games in 2017, and I have also recieved grants from the Girls of the Guild and Hilda Marindale to study my Masters degree in 2019. As of 2020 I was also awarded the Google Techmakers grant to continue studying to a PhD level.

I left main stream school at 14 as I have dyslexia and dyspraxia and falling behind in most classes compared to my peers, I was then educated by Oxford Open Learning. I worked for some time as a zookeeper before deciding to take the leap and follow my dream to becoming a game programmer.

Ski Cheap

Online game created using JavaScript. The player controls a skier using the mouse and has to avoid the trees based on the game Ski Free. I was the only person working on this game.

Street Cat Fighter

Street cat fighter is a a mock of street fighter but with cats created using Unreal Engine blueprints. I was the only developer on this game and I also created a custom controller using an arduino and a cat toy you click the paws, mouth or tail to fight the AI and win to gain territory.

Youtube

Shark Bait

Shark Bait is a couch co-op game, made with Unreal Engine blueprints. I programmed the mutiplayer system, all the fish AI and UI. I also acted as lead programmer and handed jobs to the two other programmers.

Youtube Facebook Itch.io

Standby

Standby in a stealth, horror game created using Unreal Engine blueprints. The player is trapped in thier smart home where everything is controlled by their robot butler that has turned rogue and is now trying to kill its master. I was purely the AI programmer for the team creating the enemy NPC

Slime EVO

Slime Evo created using python and pygame is a single player platformer. Created by me and three other programmers, my sole responsibility was making sure the collision and gravity worked correctly. This was the first game I made.
